Come away with us for the weekend!

We would love nothing more than for all of our friends & family to come spend the whole late September weekend with us in Lakeville, CT. We specifically chose the Interlaken Inn for its “lake resort” feel in one of the most beautiful parts of our state.

Interlaken Inn

The year-round country resort is set on 30 beautiful acres nestled between two lakes in the Litchfield Hills of Connecticut. As a resort, our venue has a two night minimum for stays, and not at your typical Holiday Inn Express prices. We really think you will find value in your choice to stay with us though, and want to encourage everyone who has it within their means to consider staying with us here. We are planning on enjoying the entire weekend in Lakeville and there will be sure to be plenty of things to do all weekend. We would even suggest grabbing a friend and splitting the cost of a room with 2 queen beds! Or a big group (3-4 couples) in the townhouse suites for a better bang for your buck!

Please note that our block of rooms will only be available through March 20, 2019!! If you think you may want to book, consider putting a hold on a room. There is a $25 cancellation fee, but it may be better than missing out!
