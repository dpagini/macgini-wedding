On Saturday, September 21st, 2019 in beautiful Lakeville, CT at the Interlaken Inn, Rebecca and I will say our “I do’s” overlooking Lake Wononskopomuc. We moved pretty quickly (in my opinion), but we were targeting next fall and knew we needed to book 12-14 months in advance. I usually get some agita over a decision like this, and need to research and measure 10 times before I cut once, but in this case, it was one of those “when you know, you know” situations (sort of like our relationship!).

We were both awe-struck by the beauty of this area, and the staff at Interlaken simply blew our socks off. We both had a vision of spending a weekend with our closest friends and family, and throwing one hell of a celebration, and we really could envision it happening here.
